Ethical Dimension

Considering values, justice, and responsible use

Potential Misuse and Harm

Identify potential ways the tool could be misused or cause harm. What safeguards exist to prevent misuse? How might the tool be weaponized or used unethically? What are the consequences for students and educators?

Privacy and Data Governance

Examine privacy implications and data governance practices. What data does the tool collect? How is it stored and used? What are the privacy policies? Are there risks to student data or educator information?

Labour and Economic Justice

Consider the broader economic and labour implications. How does the tool affect educator employment? What are the training data sources and labour practices? Who benefits economically from the tool?

Equity and Access

Assess equity and access considerations. Who has access to this tool? Are there barriers for marginalized communities? Does the tool exacerbate or reduce educational inequities?
