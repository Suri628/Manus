Critical Dimension

Examining AI reliability, accuracy, and potential harms

Hallucinations and Factual Errors

Evaluate the tool's tendency to generate plausible-sounding but incorrect information. How often does it produce hallucinations? What types of errors are most common? How can educators verify the accuracy of AI-generated content?

Bias and Fairness

Examine potential biases in the AI tool. Does it perpetuate stereotypes? Are certain groups or perspectives underrepresented? How might biased outputs affect diverse learners? What steps can mitigate these issues?

Verification and Fact-Checking

Describe strategies for verifying AI-generated content. What tools or methods can educators use? How can students develop critical evaluation skills? What role should human review play in AI-assisted workflows?

Reliability and Consistency

Assess the consistency and reliability of the tool's outputs. Does it produce similar results for similar inputs? How does performance vary across different contexts or domains?
