Metacognitive Dimension

Reflecting on learning, awareness, and critical thinking

When to Use AI and When Not To

Develop frameworks for deciding when AI tools are appropriate and when they should be avoided. What types of learning tasks benefit from AI support? When might AI undermine learning goals? How can educators and students make informed decisions about tool use?

Integration with Learning Processes

Consider how AI tools integrate with broader learning processes. Does the tool support or hinder metacognitive development? Does it encourage reflection and self-assessment? How does it affect student agency and ownership of learning?

What's Worth Learning in an AI World

Reflect on curriculum and learning priorities in the context of AI. What skills and knowledge remain essential when AI can perform many tasks? How should education evolve to prepare students for an AI-integrated world?

Developing Critical Evaluation Skills

Explore how educators and students can develop critical evaluation skills for AI tools. What frameworks support critical thinking about AI? How can we cultivate healthy skepticism and informed adoption?
