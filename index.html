A Critical Demonstration for Educators (Manus 1.5)

Created by Betty · Cathy · Suri — CTL1615H (UofT OISE)

Manus AI (from the Latin word for "hand") is the world's first fully autonomous AI agent designed to execute complex, multi-step tasks independently with minimal human supervision. Unlike traditional AI chatbots that simply provide information or suggestions, Manus AI can plan, research, execute, and deliver complete results—functioning more like a capable assistant than a passive tool.This Blog shows Manus AI as a case study to systematically analyze this new AI tool across five dimensions: pedagogical, technical, critical, ethical, and metacognitive.
